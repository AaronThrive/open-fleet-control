Per-model rows are badged "subscription" when their marginal (real) cost is $0 because the usage is covered by an OAuth / subscription plan, or "per-token" when billed incrementally. Cache reads are billed at a steep discount and cache writes at a premium, so they are shown separately and never lumped into raw input/output totals.
